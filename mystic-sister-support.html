<h2>Basic Troubleshooting</h2>

<h3>If readings are not generating:</h3>

<ul>

  <li>Ensure you have a stable internet connection.</li>

  <li>Fully close and reopen the app.</li>

  <li>Confirm your question was submitted successfully.</li>

</ul>


<h3>If subscription information is not showing correctly:</h3>

<ul>

  <li>Log out and log back in.</li>

  <li>Ensure you are using the correct account.</li>

  <li>Refresh the subscriptions page.</li>

</ul>


<h3>If visuals or layouts look incorrect:</h3>

<ul>

  <li>Restart the app.</li>

  <li>Check for the latest version in the App Store.</li>

  <li>If using TestFlight, ensure you have updated to the newest build.</li>

</ul>


<h3>If push notifications are not appearing:</h3>

<ul>

  <li>Open <strong>Settings → Notifications → Mystic Sister</strong></li>

  <li>Enable Alerts, Sounds, and Badges.</li>

</ul>

</div>
